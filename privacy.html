Privacy Policy

Analytics Information

This website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand how visitors use our site and improve our service.

What We Collect

Through Google Analytics, we collect:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Referral source

Privacy Protection

We take several measures to protect your privacy:

  • IP Anonymization is enabled
  • No personal information is collected
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • We don't use cookies for tracking

Your Business Card Data

The business card information you enter (name, contact details, etc.) is processed entirely in your browser. We do not store or collect this information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

When you click the "Print-Fedex" button, you'll be redirected to FedEx's website. Please note that FedEx has its own privacy policy and terms of service.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please feel free to contact us.
